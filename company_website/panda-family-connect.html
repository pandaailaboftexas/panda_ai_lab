Who We're Here For

Made for people who need more connection.

Loneliness affects millions of seniors and families across Canada and the United States. Our services are designed for real people in real situations — not clinical patients, but people who simply need a caring presence.

👴
Seniors Living Alone
Adults 65+ who live independently and benefit from regular, warm conversation and check-ins.
👨‍👩‍👧
Families Far from Loved Ones
Children or relatives who want their elderly parents to have consistent companionship when they can't be there.
🫂
Caregivers & Family Members
Those navigating difficult family situations who need a steady, non-judgmental listener and support space.
🌏
Chinese-Canadian Families
Fully bilingual support in English and Mandarin — bridging language and cultural barriers with ease.

Loneliness isn't just about being alone. It's about feeling like no one is really listening.

Research consistently shows that social isolation has serious effects on physical and mental health. Regular, warm conversation — even a few times a week — can meaningfully reduce loneliness and improve wellbeing.

Panda Family Connect exists to make that kind of connection accessible, affordable, and available in both English and Mandarin — serving clients across Canada and the United States.

The Process

Simple, gentle, and at your pace.

We make it easy to get started. No forms, no wait lists, no complicated processes. Just a friendly conversation to start.

1

Book a Free Intro Call

2

We Match You with a Companion

3
